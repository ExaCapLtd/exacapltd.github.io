Portfolio company [ClearStake](https://www.clearstake.com) has signed **SkillOnNet**, the operator behind PlayOJO and a portfolio of online casino brands, to implement bank-based verification technology for enhanced due diligence and source of funds checks.

The partnership marks another major operator choosing ClearStake over traditional document-heavy compliance processes. SkillOnNet will replace manual EDD workflows with ClearStake's real-time, bank-verified checks — reducing operational overhead while improving the player experience.
