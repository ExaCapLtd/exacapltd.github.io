Portfolio company [ClearStake](https://www.clearstake.com) continues to build momentum, with **Hollywoodbets** and **Star Sports** both adopting the ID by Bank verification platform in recent months.

These latest signings follow ClearStake's successful partnership with MrQ in mid-2025, and bring the platform's operator base to a growing roster of UK-regulated gaming companies including L&L Europe, tombola, and Ivy Casino.

## Why Operators Are Switching

The traditional approach to player verification — uploading passport photos, utility bills, and bank statements — creates friction that causes significant player drop-off during registration. ClearStake's ID by Bank product eliminates this entirely.

Players verify their name, address, and date of birth through their banking app in under 30 seconds, using the biometric authentication already built into their bank. For operators, this means higher conversion rates and audit-ready compliance data from day one.

When L&L Europe first integrated ClearStake's affordability checks, completion rates jumped from 10% to 60%.

## Scaling the Platform

With the commercial team growing following their £1.5m seed round in November, ClearStake is now focused on expanding across the UK regulated market and exploring adjacent verticals where open banking verification can replace manual document processes.
