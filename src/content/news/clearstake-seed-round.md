We're proud to announce that [ClearStake](https://www.clearstake.com), one of our portfolio companies, has closed a £1.5 million seed round backed by an impressive roster of iGaming industry veterans.

The round was backed by investors including Michael Brady (Bede Gaming, Conexus), Simon Collins (former Foxy Bingo, Gaming Realms), Savvas Fellas (CEO of MrQ.com), and several other prominent figures from across the gaming and fintech sectors.

## What ClearStake Does

ClearStake provides open banking-powered customer due diligence for the regulated iGaming industry. Their platform handles identity verification, affordability checks, source of funds, and AML compliance through a single API — replacing slow, manual document upload processes with real-time bank-verified signals.

Their flagship product, **ID by Bank**, allows players to verify their identity through their banking app in under 30 seconds using the biometric authentication already built into their bank.

## What This Means

The funding will be used to accelerate the rollout of ID by Bank, expand the commercial team, and deepen integrations with UK-regulated operators.

ClearStake CEO Martin Burt said: *"This raise signals our mission matters more than ever. We're proving that exceptional customer experience around verification and compliance can actually drive growth."*

As a technical partner, ExaCap continues to support ClearStake's engineering capability as they scale.
