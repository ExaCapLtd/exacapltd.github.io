We're delighted to share that [AdPeak](https://adpeak.ai), our AI-powered eCommerce advertising platform, has won **Best Use of Search - Retail/E-commerce (PPC)** at the prestigious Global Search Awards 2025.

The award recognised the campaign "From Leakage to Leverage: The PPC Fix for Plumb2U", delivered by ExaDev, 94n Digital, and Supplyant — with AdPeak as the underlying technology powering the results.

Judges described the work as *"Outstanding, data-driven strategy with transparent objectives and multi-layered, profit-focused campaign structure."*

## The Results

The AdPeak platform delivered transformative results for Plumb2U, a home improvement retailer:

- **37.9%** year-over-year revenue increase
- **£343,000** additional revenue in three months
- Plumb2U's **best trading month in five years**
- Zero-click products reduced from **80% to 26.5%**

## How AdPeak Works

AdPeak uses AI to automate and optimise Google Ads for large eCommerce catalogues. Its smart product clustering groups items by performance tiers, ensuring new and underperforming products get fair testing rather than being starved of budget. Stock-aware optimisation syncs with Merchant Center in real time to avoid wasting spend on out-of-stock items.

Every optimisation is presented as a reviewable change plan with automatic rollback — giving agencies and brands full visibility and control.
