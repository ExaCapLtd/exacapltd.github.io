Hot on the heels of their Global Search Awards win in October, [AdPeak](https://adpeak.ai) has picked up another major industry award — **Best Use of Data (PPC)** at the UK Search Awards 2025.

The award was given for 94n Digital's campaign work with Plumb2U, powered by AdPeak's AI advertising platform. This marks the second major award in two months for the AdPeak technology stack.

## A Pattern of Recognition

AdPeak was also shortlisted as a finalist for **Paid Media Campaign of the Year** at the European Paid Media Awards 2025, further cementing the platform's reputation as a serious force in AI-driven eCommerce advertising.
