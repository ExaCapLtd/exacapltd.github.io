In a landmark moment for portfolio company [ClearStake](https://www.clearstake.com), **Paddy Power** has partnered with ClearStake to roll out ID by Bank technology across its retail betting shops — making the Flutter-owned brand the first major high street bookmaker to adopt bank-based identity verification.

## What This Means

Paddy Power shop staff will now be able to verify customer identity and perform source of funds checks instantly via a banking app, replacing the slow, manual KYC processes that have frustrated retail customers for years.

The deal is significant for several reasons:

- **First retail deployment** — ID by Bank was originally built for online operators. This extends ClearStake's reach into the brick-and-mortar betting sector.
- **Flutter scale** — Paddy Power is part of Flutter Entertainment, the world's largest online gambling company. A successful rollout here opens the door to Flutter's other brands.
- **Investor alignment** — Paddy Power was among the investors in ClearStake's £1.5m seed round in late 2025, making this a natural progression from financial backer to commercial partner.

## Growing Momentum

This announcement comes during a period of rapid customer acquisition for ClearStake. In recent months, the platform has signed Hollywoodbets, Star Sports, and SkillOnNet (PlayOJO), adding to existing partnerships with MrQ, tombola, L&L Europe, and Ivy Casino.

ClearStake is proving that frictionless compliance isn't just a nice-to-have — it's a competitive advantage that drives higher conversion rates and better customer experience across both online and retail channels.
